# Network connectivity issues related to outdated OS

## Problem
Customer states they are having issues with their device being unable to connect to their WiFi network

## Investigation
- Verified issue by testing device with good known WiFi network, device unable to connect/scan for available networks. 
- Ran diagnostics: no hardware issues present. 
- Identified issue is most likely due to software. 
- Informed customer of importance of having data backup and possibility of data loss if backup isn't present. 

## Root Cause
Device exhibiting unexpected behavior due to device being on a much older software version. 

## Resolution
- Connected device to internet via Ethernet cable. 
- Proceeded to update device to latest MacOS. 
- Issue still persistent due to possible corrupted device data. 
- Proceeded to boot device into recovery mode. 
- Reinstalled MacOS. 
- Device now able to scan for and connect to available WiFi networks. 
- Restored user data from backup. 

## Preventative Measures 
Educated user on importance of continuously updating device OS and best practices for updates/backups.
